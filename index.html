Guide

Welcome to the Calendar 0.60. This app its a way to confirm that you are ok with the 60% of the office days in case that you find yourself lost


How its works?

  • First of all, the calendar suppose that you work from Mondays to Fridays. So it will initially calculate the 60% of them.

  • Left click to select a Office Day.

  • Right click to select a Licence Day.(Not included when do the 60%)

  • (Tip) You can select the name of the day and all the days of the month with that name (e.g. Monday) will be selected
