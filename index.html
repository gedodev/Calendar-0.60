Guide

Welcome to the Calendar 0.60. This app it's a way to confirm that you are ok with the 60% of the office days in case that you find yourself lost


How its works?

  • First of all, the calendar suppose that you work from Mondays to Fridays. So it will initially calculate the 60% of them and compare with the days that you go to the office

  • Left click to select a Office Day.

  • Right click to select a Licence Day.(The Study and Sick Licence are included here and all of them are not included in the 100% of days worked)

  • (Tip 1) You can click in the name of a day (e.g. Monday) and it will select all the days of that day name (e.g. All the mondays in January)

  • (Tip 2) You can select a range of days, dragging the click through the calendar
